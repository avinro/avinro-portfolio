Aurora is a concept fintech app exploring how to make the first 5 minutes — from download to first transaction — feel effortless without sacrificing the security signals users need to trust a new banking product.

The primary design challenge was onboarding: identity verification typically spans two sessions (document scan + selfie), creates a 40% drop-off window, and leaves users uncertain whether the app actually works. The design condenses this into a single guided session with real-time feedback at every step.
